双语阅读内容

Learning a new language is like embarking on a journey to a new world. It opens doors to different cultures, perspectives, and ways of thinking. Persistence坚持
词根: persist (坚持) + ence (名词后缀)
例句: Her persistence in studying paid off when she passed the exam. is key to mastering any language.

学习一门新语言就像踏上通往新世界的旅程。它为不同的文化、观点和思维方式打开了大门。坚持是掌握任何语言的关键。

The acquisition习得，获得
词根: acquire (获得) + tion (名词后缀)
例句: The acquisition of knowledge is a lifelong process. of vocabulary is fundamental. Each word is a building block that helps construct sentences and express ideas. Reading books, watching movies, and engaging in conversations are excellent ways to enhance提高，增强
词根: en (使) + hance (高)
例句: Regular practice will enhance your language skills. your language skills.

词汇的习得是基础。每个单词都是帮助构建句子和表达想法的基石。阅读书籍、观看电影和参与对话是提高语言技能的绝佳方式。

Don't be intimidated感到恐惧的，被吓倒的
词根: in (使) + timid (胆怯的) + ate (使) + d (过去分词)
例句: Don't be intimidated by difficult grammar rules. by mistakes; they are stepping stones to fluency. Every error is an opportunity to learn and improve. The journey of language learning is not about reaching a destination but enjoying the process of growth and discovery.

不要被错误吓倒；它们是通向流利的垫脚石。每个错误都是学习和提高的机会。语言学习的旅程不是为了到达目的地，而是享受成长和发现的过程。
